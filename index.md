Typical use cases
---
High-performance audio is required in many applications. Examples include:

- Digital Audio Workstations (DAWs)
- Synthesizers
- Drum machines
- Music learning apps
- Karaoke apps
- DJ mixing
- Audio effects
- Video/Audio conferencing
